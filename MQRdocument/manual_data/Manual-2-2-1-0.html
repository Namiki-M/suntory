1.3.2.1　General requirement
a.　Check on receiving
Defect mode Organoleptic defect / Microbe contamination / Foreign material contamination / Sealing defect / Packaging appearance defect / Allergen contamination
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To reject receiving raw materials and packaging that are non-conforming or were incorrectly delivered.
What/how to achieve

◆Receive "certificates required by the agreement" (origin certificate, inspection summary report, loading certificate, etc.) issued by the supplier and/or carrier when incoming products are received. They can be obtained as electronic data in advance.

 　

◆Specify the method to check the certificates on receiving to find any wrong incoming products. (See the figure.)

 　

◆Specify the operation method to check the transport vehicle and incoming products on receiving to prevent any defective products from being received. (See the figure.)

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Make sure that the description in the certificates matches the specifications and standards. See the figure for the check items. 　
Make sure that the description in the shipment document matches the order description. 　
Frequency or timing Every receiving 　
Operating limit or standard condition Based on the raw material and packaging specifications and standards 　
Task 2
Procedure Check the transport vehicle on receiving for any strange odors. See the figure for the check items. 　
Frequency or timing Every receiving 　
Operating limit or standard condition The vehicle must be clean. No strange odors. (It is desirable that incoming products should not be loaded with non-food items.) 　
Task 3
Procedure Check the condition of incoming products. 　
Frequency or timing Every receiving 　
Operating limit or standard condition No damage or leaking. The storage conditions defined in the specifications must be met. 　
Figure
b.　Sampling on receiving
Defect mode Organoleptic defect / Microbe contamination / Different packaging / Sealing defect / Scratched packaging which may cause injury / Packaging appearance defect
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To check raw materials and packaging in incoming inspection with sampling to ensure that they comply with the laws and regulations and the specifications set by corresponding companies.
What/how to achieve

◆Confirm correspondence between the raw material or packaging label against the standard sample according to the specifications.

 　

◆Specify the sampling work environment and procedure to prevent cross-contamination of a sample and raw materials/packaging.

 　

◆Specify the raw materials and primary packaging which microbe test and sensory test is needed, and specify it's evaluation method.

 　
　

・In general, perform sampling from incoming products for the microbe test and the sensory test. (Do not use a pre-ship sample.)

 　

◆Specify the evaluation method after checking the necessity of the physical/chemical test, physical property test, intensity test, and visual inspection according to the raw material and packaging specifications.

 　

◆Perform the quality test if it is required by the laws and regulations.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Perform sampling inspection according to the predetermined items and procedures. 　
Frequency or timing For each transport vehicle for bulk materials
Based on the frequency set locally for other materials		 　
Operating limit or standard condition Based on the inspection standards set for each raw material or packaging 　
Task 2
Procedure Make sure that the inspection facility and inspectors have the required capabilities. 　
Frequency or timing Set locally
Be sure to perform when the inspection facility or inspectors are changed.		 　
Operating limit or standard condition Based on the inspection standards set for each raw material or packaging
