1.3.11.3　Hot filling/nitro hot filling
a.　General requirement for hot filling
Defect mode Microbe contamination / Organoleptic defect / Sealing defect / Packaging appearance defect
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line Hot filling
Purpose
To fill at a contents temperature appropriate for packaging sterilization.
What/how to achieve

◆The filling temperature defined in the product specification must be met.

 　

◆Set an interlock to trigger an alarm and stop filling when the temperature inside the filler bowl is outside the operating range.

 　

◆Be sure to measure the temperature of actual products because it gets lower at the start or restart of filling. However, this does not apply to a short-time stop, which has been verified to cause no effects on a drop in temperature at the restart of filling.

◆Store important equipment appropriately that measures the product temperature. (See the figure.)

 　

◆Products that remain between filler and tilting conveyor must be disposed of or handled according to the rule This is to prevent sealing defects, temperature drop, or packaging deformation. (See the figure.)
　

・It is desirable to set an interlock to trigger an alarm and stop transferring when the predetermined retention time passes.

 　

◆Dispose of the filled products that fall or drop before cooling process.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Open the bottle and measure the product temperature. 　
Frequency or timing Right after the start of filling, first bottle after a long-time stoppage, and at the frequency set locally during operation (Example: Every hour)
Be sure to measure the temperature immediately after picking out products at the predetermined location.		 　
Operating limit or standard condition The operating limit in the product specification must be met. 　
Figure
Details
Hot filling process is designed to pasteurize microbes by heat of in-bottle products. So the proper in-bottle temperature must be achieved, or it may result in defective pasteurization and spoilage.
b.　General requirement for tilting conveyor sterilization
Defect mode Microbe contamination / Organoleptic defect / Sealing defect / Packaging appearance defect
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line Hot filling
Purpose
To perform sterilization defined in the product specification on the entire inner surface of packaging which can contact with contents. See the background. (See the background.)
What/how to achieve

◆Set transfer conditions to bring contents into contact with the inner surface of packaging.

 　
　

・Products must be tilted and rocked to prevent sterilization defects due to an air pocket (For bottles, it is desirable to tilt 100 degrees or more). (See the figure.)

 　
　

・Set the temperature at the end of tilting conveyor sterilization and set the duration time of tilting which depends on SKU but is usually about 20 to 40 seconds. For pouches, the time must be set to prevent packaging deformation.

 　
　

・Since flexible packaging, such as pouches, is difficult to be completely sterilized by tilting conveyor sterilization, try other sterilization methods as necessary. (See the figure.)　(Steam lifter, etc.)

 　

◆If filled products remain in tilting conveyor sterilization, dispose of or appropriately handle them according to the set rules In order to prevent sealing defects, temperature drop, or packaging deformation. (See the figure.)
　

・It is desirable to set an interlock to trigger an alarm and stop transferring when the predetermined retention time passes.

 　

◆Even when bottles remain in the cooling tunnel, shower cooling must be continued until the cooling reference temperature is reached.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Specify the sampling position and check the temperature of products after tilting conveyor sterilization. 　
Check the transfer time, tilt angle, and rocking state of tilting conveyor sterilization. 　
Frequency or timing Immediately after the start or restart of filling and at the frequency set locally during operation (Example: Every four hours) 　
Operating limit or standard condition Set locally 　
Figure
Background
Flexible packaging such as a pouch has an unstable shape, so it easily causes air entrapment. Therefore, select an area that a liquid does not fully reach and is subject to a temperature drop, such as a pouch joint, for the sterilization condition and then perform evaluation and verification.
The PU calculation formula is as follows: PU = 19 or above is desired.
PU = t x 10 (T - Tref) / z, t = Liquid-contact time (min), T = Liquid-contact temperature, Tref = 60 ℃, Z = 6 ℃
Assuming that sterilization of 6D or above is performed on general mold A. niger (D70 = 4 seconds, Z = 6), PU is set to 70 ℃ and 24 seconds or above.
Details
The packaging is not sterile prior to the filling operation. To decrease microbes from the packaging, hot beverages must contact all interior surfaces of the packaging for a specific amount of time. Packaging inversion ensures that a hot beverage contacts the entire surface of the closure. 　
The inversion time is an important parameter because shorter inversion time leads to lack of pasteurization of closure surfaces. On the contrary, an excessive inversion time in the inverted position causes damage to the neck of the bottle and leads to inadequate sealing.
c.　Nitro hot filling (inner pressure retention)
Defect mode Microbe contamination / Organoleptic defect / Sealing defect / Packaging appearance defect
Subjected packaging PET
Subjected line Hot filling
Purpose
To prevent insufficient inner pressure of products when adding nitrogen in hot filling.
(Note) This section describes the requirements for nitro hot filling. For the hot filling general requirements, see "Hot filling" and "Tilting conveyor sterilization".
What/how to achieve

◆To maintain positive pressure beyond the best before dates, set the standards for the inner pressure of products after filling.

 　

◆Set the conditions for adding nitrogen.

 　
　

・Add nitrogen into a product according to the filling speed. (Example: At a low or high speed)

 　
　

・The nozzle position of the adding machine and the container transfer position must be centered.

 　

◆After maintenance on the nitrogen adding machine, specify the procedures to check the nitrogen adding status (spray position, pressure, flow rate, etc.) and the reproduction of inner pressure of products.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Specify the sampling position and check the inner pressure of products after filling. For the notes on the inner pressure inspection, see 1.3.11.1.d. 　
Frequency or timing After the start or restart of filling for the first valve and at the frequency set locally afterward (Example: Every hour) 　
Operating limit or standard condition Set locally 　
Task 2
Procedure Check the inner pressure of products after cooling. 　
Frequency or timing Set locally (Example: Every hour) 　
Operating limit or standard condition Set locally 　
Task 3
Procedure Periodically check the nitrogen adding conditions (liquid nitrogen level in the adding machine, adding time, timing with containers, etc.). 　
Frequency or timing Set locally (after changing the type, at start, scheduled check during operation, etc.) 　
Operating limit or standard condition Set locally 　
Figure
Background
PET nitro hot filling line provides products with buckling strength by positive pressurization inside products. Sterilization of the connections between caps and bottle neck with hot water shower can be omitted, so the positive pressure inside products must be maintained until the best before dates expire. A packaging shape must be designed to withstand inner pressure, but the packaging bottoms need to be cooled to prevent deformation due to heated contents without affecting products sterilization.

Nitro hot filling does not require a neck shape that is crystallized or non-crystal-enhanced in consideration of preform heat resistance because hot water shower is not applied or positive pressure is maintained inside containers unlike normal hot filling. Therefore, deformation or sealing defects due to heat from the necks must be prevented in the transfer process after filling until cooling is completed.
d.　Nitro hot filling (container deformation prevention)
Defect mode Microbe contamination / Organoleptic defect / Sealing defect / Packaging appearance defect
Subjected packaging PET
Subjected line Hot filling
Purpose
To prevent sealing defects or container deformation when adding nitrogen in hot filling.
(Note) This section describes the requirements for nitro hot filling. For the hot filling general requirements, see "Hot filling" and "Tilting conveyor sterilization".
What/how to achieve

◆To prevent bottom deformation (buckling), cool the bottom of a product with water after capping before cooling tunnel.

 　
　

・Specify the spray position, water temperature, flow rate, pressure, and spray time of cooling water that do not affect the temperature of contents for container sterilization.

 　
　

・Specify the retention time of product on a conveyor in consideration of a drop of contents temperature due to bottom cooling.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Check the spraying state, water temperature, and flow rate of cooling water applied to the bottoms of the containers. 　
Frequency or timing Set locally (after changing the type, at start, scheduled check during operation, etc.) 　
Operating limit or standard condition Set locally
