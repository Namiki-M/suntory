1.3.6.4　Measuring instruments (thermometer/manometer)
a.　General requirement for measuring instruments
Defect mode Organoleptic defect / Microbe contamination / Others (violation of law)
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To choose, install, and maintain appropriate measuring instruments for proper production processes
What/how to achieve

◆Appropriate measuring instruments must be chosen in consideration of accuracy, measurable decimal digits, and significant figures for each monitoring target. (For measuring instrument examples, see the details.)

 　

◆Install a measuring instrument in a location appropriate for the monitoring purpose.

 　

◆Specify the maintenance frequency and method for each measuring instrument. Observe the calibration frequency if it is set by the laws.
　

・Calibrate important measuring instruments, such as CCP, at least once a year.

 　
　

・List measuring instruments so that all of them are correctly calibrated and centrally manage the calibration schedule dates and records. (See the figure.)

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Perform calibration and maintenance according to the rules. 　
Frequency or timing Set locally in accordance with the laws and regulations 　
Operating limit or standard condition The accuracy must be set for each instrument locally and observed. 　
Figure
Details
Measuring instrument example:
Thermometer, manometer, room thermometer, recording device
Chlorine concentration monitor, alarm device
Weighing device
Flowmeter
Tank weighing device (load cell, pressure converter, manometer, etc.)
Pump manometer
Refractometer, CO2 manometer
b.　Prevention of mixing different liquid
Defect mode Mixing different liquid
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To prevent health hazard caused by a liquid contained in a measuring instrument being mixed in a contents liquid.
What/how to achieve

◆Do not install a glass thermometer (bar thermometer), which contains a liquid such as mercury, directly on the pipes for contents, raw materials, or contents water.

 　

◆It is desirable to choose a manometer that contains no liquids.

 　

◆If a manometer contains a liquid, it must be food grade.

 　
　

・Specify the frequency to check the diaphragm for any cracks and replace it if is abnormal.
