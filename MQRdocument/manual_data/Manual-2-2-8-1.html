1.3.9.2　Packaging/product transfer
a.　Prevention of foreign material contamination during transfer
Defect mode Foreign material contamination / Packaging appearance defect / Microbe contamination
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To prevent foreign materials or different liquids from being mixed in or sticking to primary packaging before filling or sealing
To prevent microbe contamination during transfer
What/how to achieve

◆To keep the packaging insert equipment (PF inverter, can depalletizer, can lid unwrapper, cap inserter, etc.) clean, it is desirable to increase the cleanliness of a room with the insert equipment higher than other rooms or enclose the insert equipment itself.

 　

◆The equipment to transfer pre-sealed primary packaging must be enclosed or covered to protect foreign materials and insects. See the figure (Grease and living insects cannot be completely removed by a rinser).

 　

◆To transfer packaging using compressed air, use a filter with air cleanliness appropriate for each filling method.

 　
　

・Example: Hot filling, CSD: MEPA or above (Air filter with particle collection efficiency of about 60 to 95 % mainly for particles with a diameter of 5 μm or less)

 　
　

・Example: Aseptic line: HEPA (Air filter with particle collection efficiency of 99.97 % or above for particles with a diameter of 0.3 μm and an initial pressure drop of 245 Pa or less in the rated air flow)

 　

◆The sorter of caps or crown corks must be stopped when the line is stopped. (This is because metal or resin chips are generated in the sorter, which leads to foreign material contamination. See the figure.)

 　
　

・A dust remover must be installed after the sorter.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Clean the air filter, air outlet, cap hopper, sorter, chute, and conveyor cover. (See the figure.) 　
Check the conveyor cover for any damage or cracks that may lead to foreign material contamination. 　
Frequency or timing Set locally 　
Operating limit or standard condition No dirt/dust or damage 　
Task 2
Procedure Check the pressure of the dust remover if it is installed. 　
Frequency or timing Before starting production 　
Operating limit or standard condition The pressure operating limit must be set locally according to the equipment.

b.　Prevention of damage during transfer
Defect mode Sealing defect / Packaging appearance defect / Foreign material contamination
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To prevent packaging from being damaged during insert or transfer.
To prevent damage to the opening of packaging particularly
What/how to achieve

◆Avoid contact between packaging and hard parts. (Example: Depalletizer seat gripper, etc.)

 　

◆Set transfer conditions to prevent damage to packaging. (Example: Drop/collision during preform transfer, accumulated pressure of the preform and cap, strength of transfer air, conveyor speed, conveyor guide width/step (reverse step), etc.)

 　
Figure
c.　Prevention of falls during conveyor transfer
Defect mode Sealing defect / Packaging appearance defect
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To prevent falls or appearance defects due to lubricant during conveyor transfer
What/how to achieve

◆Specify an appropriate conveyor speed to prevent products from falling.

 　

◆To prevent products from falling, the step between conveyors or guides must be level or forward (upstream side at a higher position). (See the figure.)

 　
　

・Set a fall prevention guide for moving products onto the conveyor. (See the figure.)

 　

◆The guide must not be tilted in a vertical direction.

 　

◆Appearance defects on products due to excessive lubricant must be prevented.

 　

◆It is basically desirable to dispose of fallen packaging/products. (This is because fallen packaging/products cannot guarantee the sealing performance.)

 　

◆For glass bottle transfer, also see the appropriate chapter.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Check the amount of conveyor lubricant and the transfer state of products. 　
Frequency or timing Before starting production 　
Operating limit or standard condition An excessive amount of lubricant must not be used (See the figure). No falls. No contact between products. No unstably tilted product at the screw conveyor. 　
Task 2
Procedure Perform change over for the guide according to the SOP. (No tilted guide or reverse step) 　
Frequency or timing At change over 　
Operating limit or standard condition The limit/condition must comply with the standards. 　
Task 3
Procedure Perform maintenance on the transfer equipment (inter-conveyor step check, guide/conveyor replacement, rail/frame leveling, etc.). 　
Frequency or timing Set locally for each equipment 　
Operating limit or standard condition Set locally 　
Figure
d.　Removal of residual packaging (prevention of different packaging)
Defect mode Sealing defect / Packaging appearance defect / Different packaging
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To remove packaging from the line at the end of production or when changing SKU to prevent it from being mixed in products to be produced next
What/how to achieve

◆In each equipment, clarify areas that need to be checked for any residual packaging and standardize the removal work.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Remove residual packaging from the production line. 　
Frequency or timing At the end of production, when changing production SKU, when the production line has been stopped for a long time (Example: 24 hours or longer) 　
Operating limit or standard condition No residual packaging 　
Figure
e.　Glass bottle transfer
Defect mode Sealing defect / Foreign material contamination
Subjected packaging Glass bottle
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To prevent bottles from being damaged or broken pieces from being mixed in products during glass bottle transfer
What/how to achieve

◆To prevent bottles from colliding with each other due to excessive impact, specify the conveyor speed and the speed check frequency.

 　

◆Install a glass shattering prevention cover on each screw conveyor or between star wheels.

 　

◆It is desirable to prevent jamming in the later process by introducing a device to detect broken and fallen bottles and stop the equipment or remove the bottles. (See the backgrounds for the installation location and the detection mechanism.)

 　

◆It must be controlled to stop the conveyor when a glass bottle is jammed.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure When a bottle is broken, stop the equipment and clean the shattering location according to the SOP. 　
For how to handle broken bottles in the filler, capper, or crowner, see the glass bottle filling chapter. 　
Frequency or timing When a bottle is broken 　
Operating limit or standard condition In accordance with the SOP 　
Task 2
Procedure If a broken bottle is jammed in the screw conveyor, the damaged screw conveyor may damage products. Therefore, check thoroughly before restarting the production. 　
Frequency or timing When a broken bottle is jammed in the screw conveyor 　
Operating limit or standard condition The broken bottle must be securely removed, and the equipment must not be damaged.
