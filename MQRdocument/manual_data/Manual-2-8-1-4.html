1.9.2.5　Pouch
a.　Appearance and peel-off test
Defect mode Sealing defect / Difficult to open / Packaging appearance defect / Scratched packaging which may cause injury
Subjected packaging Pouch
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
For the pouch products, confirm appropriately that the appearance and inner surface of the packaging are free from quality defects
What/how to achieve
b.　Dimension measurement
Defect mode Sealing defect / Difficult to open
Subjected packaging Pouch
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
Confirm appropriately that the dimensions of the pouch products are according to the specification
What/how to achieve
c.　Capping angle
Defect mode Sealing defect / Difficult to open
Subjected packaging Pouch
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
For the pouch products, confirm sealing performance of the caps appropriately
What/how to achieve
d.　Removal torque
Defect mode Sealing defect / Difficult to open
Subjected packaging Pouch
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
For the pouch products, confirm opening performance of the caps appropriately
What/how to achieve
e.　Leak test
Defect mode Sealing defect / Difficult to open
Subjected packaging Pouch
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
Confirm appropriately that the pouch products are free from leaks
What/how to achieve
f.　Heat seal strength and heat resistance test
Defect mode Sealing defect / Difficult to open
Subjected packaging Pouch
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
For the pouch products, confirm sealing performance of the packaging heat-sealing, and heat tolerance of the products after filling appropriately
What/how to achieve
g.　Drop test
Defect mode Sealing defect
Subjected packaging Pouch
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
Confirm the sealing performance against drop impact.
What/how to achieve
