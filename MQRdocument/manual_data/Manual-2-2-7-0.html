1.3.8.1　Blow molding
a.　Quality management of blow molding bottles
Defect mode Sealing defect / Packaging appearance defect / Low or excessive filling / Others
Subjected packaging PET
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To perform blow molding under appropriate conditions to prevent bottle sealing defects or appearance defects
What/how to achieve

◆Verify and set appropriate blow molding parameters for each pair of preform and bottle shape

 　

◆Pay attention to the bottle neck because they may deform due to high temperature, causing sealing defects. (See the figure.)

 　

◆Greases and oils used in areas where they may be mixed in products must be already approved by the FDA.

 　

◆To stabilize blow molding, control the humidity and temperature of the blow molding room and adjust the temperature of preforms as necessary.

 　

◆Even for preforms, it is desirable to standardize the operation to inspect the appearance, weight, and neck size.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Check the blow molding parameters during operation. 　
Frequency or timing Set locally (Example: Continuous monitoring, every eight hours) 　
Operating limit or standard condition Within the setting range 　
Task 2
Procedure Check the quality of blow-molded bottles for all the molds. 　
Example: Container appearance visual inspection (material folding, whitened bottles, off-center gates) total height, body diameter, container full volume, section weight, buckling strength (empty bottles, products), neck inner diameter, etc. 　
Frequency or timing At start, when changing the preform or mold, at the frequency set locally during production (Example: Every eight hours) 　
Operating limit or standard condition Within the material specifications. No appearance defects (See the figure.) 　
Task 3
Procedure Make sure visually that there is no damage on the mandrel. (This is because damage on the neck inner side may affect the sealing performance. See the background.) 　
Frequency or timing Set locally 　
Operating limit or standard condition No damage

b.　Utility quality
Defect mode Microbe contamination / Mixing different liquid / Packaging appearance defect
Subjected packaging PET
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To prevent quality defects due to utilities such as cooling water or compressor air
What/how to achieve

◆It is desirable to use cooling water that hardly causes any scale.

 　

◆To prevent mixing different liquid, it is desirable to use an oil-free air compressor.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Make sure that there is no leaking of cooling water or hot water and check the temperature and flow rate. 　
Frequency or timing Set locally 　
Operating limit or standard condition No leaking or temperature/flow rate abnormalities
