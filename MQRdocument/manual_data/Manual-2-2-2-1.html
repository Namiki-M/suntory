1.3.3.2　Individual raw materials/packaging
a.　Liquid sweetening
Defect mode Organoleptic defect / Microbe contamination / Mixing different liquid / Foreign material contamination
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To prevent liquid sweetening from deteriorating during storage.
What/how to achieve

◆The storage location must be chosen to prevent flavor contamination.

 　

◆The storage temperature and the expiration date must be controlled according to the specifications of liquid sweetening.
　

・Install a heater or warmer as necessary.

 　

◆The tanks and pipes must be sanitary and washable (See "Contents transfer, liquid treatment").

 　
　

・Specify the cleaning method for the equipment and periodically clean it.

 　
　

・Specify the procedures to check residual water after washing and measure Brix when receiving liquid sweetening.

 　
　

・It is desirable to install a UV sterilizer inside the storage equipment to prevent microbes from growing.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Make sure that there is no storage deterioration in sampling inspection (Brix, color, sensory, and microbe tests). 　
Check if any crystallization is present in the storage equipment. 　
Frequency or timing Based on the frequency set locally 　
Operating limit or standard condition Within the specifications 　
Task 2
Procedure Maintain and manage the UV sterilizer appropriately. (See the UV sterilizer chapter.) 　
Frequency or timing 　 　
Operating limit or standard condition 　 　
b.　Raw milk
Defect mode Microbe contamination
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To appropriately manage raw milk, which has a high health hazard risk due to microbe contamination.
What/how to achieve

◆The handling, storage, and sanitation management of raw milk must comply with the laws and regulations.

 　

◆The sanitation conditions (CIP, SIP, etc.) of the raw milk tank and liquid flow parts must be set and standardized based on the validation.

◆Set the retention time in the raw milk tank and dispose of the milk appropriately when the time expires.
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Wash the raw milk tank and the liquid flow parts after use. 　
Frequency or timing After discharging the raw milk and at the frequency set locally 　
Operating limit or standard condition Washing must be completed. 　
Task 2
Procedure Measure the temperature of the raw milk and perform the sensory test after receiving it into the raw milk tank. 　
Frequency or timing Before using, once a day 　
Operating limit or standard condition Within the predetermined standard value range (Example: Temperature of 7 ℃ or below) 　
Task 3
Procedure Manage the retention time in the raw milk tank. 　
Frequency or timing Always 　
Operating limit or standard condition Within the predetermined standard value range (Example: 48 hours or less basically, within 72 hours) 　
c.　Resin caps
Defect mode Sealing defect / Difficult to open / Organoleptic defect / Scratched packaging which may cause injury / Microbe contamination
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To store resin caps at a temperature that does not cause sealing defects or make it difficult to open
What/how to achieve

◆The storage temperature of resin caps must be set according to the packaging type, combination, and filling/capping methods.

 　
　

・Set the storage temperature so that the temperature of resin caps that do not use a rinser falls into 20 to 40 ℃ when capping.

　

・Warm resin caps at least 24 hours before use.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Measure and control the storage temperature. 　
Frequency or timing Based on the frequency set locally 　
Operating limit or standard condition Value set locally 　
d.　Can lids
Defect mode Sealing defect
Subjected packaging Can
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To store can lids to prevent sealing defects (corrosion of the scoring part due to high temperature/humidity).
What/how to achieve

◆Can lids must be stored in a location with appropriate temperature and humidity. Do not expose them to humidity particularly.

 　

◆Set the expiration date to prevent the corrosion of the scoring part.
