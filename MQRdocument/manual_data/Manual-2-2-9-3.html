1.3.10.4　UV sterilizers for packaging
a.　UV sterilizers for packaging
Defect mode Microbe contamination / Foreign material contamination / Sealing defect / Difficult to open / Packaging appearance defect
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To use a UV sterilizer as necessary to prevent microbe contamination from packaging.
To prevent foreign material contamination due to damage to the UV sterilizer.
What/how to achieve

◆Choose the strength and irradiation time of ultraviolet rays according to the packaging type or the microbe risk.

 　

◆Set the sterilization conditions and the lamp position in consideration of the distance from the UV sterilization lamp and the shadow (an area that is difficult to irradiate) generated by the packaging shape.

 　

◆It is desirable that the irradiation time of ultraviolet rays and the lighting state of the UV sterilization lamp should be monitored always and interlocked.

 　

◆Install a static eliminating air dust collector to remove dust attached to packaging as necessary.

 　

◆Appropriate measures must be taken to prevent hard glass piece contamination or contained liquid contamination/exposure due to damage to the UV sterilization lamp. Pay extra attention, especially when a mercury lamp is used.

 　

◆Prevent packaging deterioration due to excessive irradiation ultraviolet rays.

 　
　

・Install an automatic circuit breaker and a return mechanism to prevent an excessive irradiation time when the packaging transfer equipment is stopped.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Make sure that the UV sterilization lamp is ON when in use. 　
Make sure that the dust collector and the static eliminator are running. 　
Frequency or timing Every production 　
Operating limit or standard condition The equipment must be turned ON and operating. No damage 　
Task 2
Procedure Periodically replace the UV sterilization lamp (including the cover) based on the total operating time. 　
Measure the UV illumination at a fixed position if possible. (See the figure.) 　
Frequency or timing Set locally (according to the frequency recommended by the manufacturer) 　
Operating limit or standard condition Set locally (according to the frequency recommended by the manufacturer) 　
Task 3
Procedure Inspect and clean the surface of the UV sterilization lamp (including the cover) periodically. If chemicals are used, choose ones appropriate for the machine. 　
Frequency or timing Set locally (according to the frequency recommended by the manufacturer) 　
Operating limit or standard condition Set locally (according to the frequency recommended by the manufacturer)
