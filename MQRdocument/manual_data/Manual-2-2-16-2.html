1.3.17.3　Inline visual inspection
a.　General requirement for inline visual inspection
Defect mode Different packaging / Low or excessive filling / Lack of products in secondary packaging / Sealing defect / BBD coding defect / Foreign material contamination
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To assure that inspection targets are securely picked out when inspecting all products visually.
What/how to achieve

◆Clearly standardize the quality defect mode, inspection targets, and inspection standards in visual inspection and visualize them in the inspection area.

 　

◆Trained persons must perform visual inspection and be periodically checked for skills in an exam.

 　

◆Set the line speed so that inspectors can perform inspection. (Example: 150 bpm or below)

 　

◆Rotate inspectors at appropriate intervals and provide a break for them. (Example: Relocating every 15 minutes, giving a 15-minute break every 60 minutes, etc.)

 　

◆An appropriate inspection environment must be maintained. (See the figure.)

 　

◆Divide inspection items and inspection areas according to the number of inspection items and assign inspectors to them. (See the figure.)

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Perform a skill exam for inspectors. 　
Frequency or timing Set locally (Example: At least once a year) 　
Operating limit or standard condition The target defects must be detected.
