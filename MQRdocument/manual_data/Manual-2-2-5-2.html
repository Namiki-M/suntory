1.3.6.3　Pumps
a.　Pump selection
Defect mode Organoleptic defect / Mixing different liquid / Foreign material contamination / Microbe contamination
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To choose an appropriate pump for operation management and maintenance (The requirements for preventing foreign metal contamination are described in the next section.)
What/how to achieve

◆In sterile areas, use an aseptic pump that prevents non-sterile outside air from contacting with a sterile liquid. (See the figure.)

 　

◆Do not use a rotary pump unless it is necessary because this equipment requires high operation/management accuracy to prevent foreign material contamination. (See the figure for a selection flow example.) Under high-load conditions, such as UHT particularly, use a centrifugal pump if possible.

 　

◆If a diaphragm pump is used, take appropriate measures to prevent oil from being mixed in contents, raw materials, or contents water when it is damaged. (Choose the oil-less or double diaphragm type, etc.)

 　
　

・If oil is used, choose food-additive-grade one.

 　

◆To prevent contents, raw materials, or contents water to be pumped from leaking, inspect and replace mechanical seals by specifying the frequency and method.

 　
Figure
b.　Prevention of foreign metal contamination
Defect mode Foreign material contamination
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To prevent pump-related foreign metal from being generated (Generation itself must be avoided because generated metal chips are too small to be caught by a strainer.)
What/how to achieve

◆Specify the maintenance frequency and method.

 　
　

・Keep an appropriate clearance so that the rotor and impeller do not contact with the housing. Turn the impeller manually to confirm no contact.

 　
　

・Re-tighten the rotor and impeller nuts. Make sure that no bearings are wobbling.

 　

◆To prevent air entrainment, maintain the liquid level required in the tank before pumping and start pumping after the valve is opened.

 　

◆If a rotary pump is used, the following must be noted on the design to prevent foreign metal contamination:

 　
　

・Install a filter before the pump to remove foreign materials that may cause entrainment in the pump.

 　
　

・To prevent vibration due to a pressure rise, release the pressure by opening the valve after the pump or installing a safety valve.

 　
　

・To prevent cavitation, a pipeline configuration must be designed appropriately to secure the pressure on the suction side (NPSH design).

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Make sure that there is no abnormal noise or vibration. 　
Frequency or timing Set locally (at least once a day during operation) 　
Operating limit or standard condition No abnormalities
