1.2.1.2　General requirement
a.　Compliance with laws and regulations
Defect mode Usage of restricted ingredients/packaging
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
Comply with local laws and regulations.
What/how to achieve

◆The quality of all packaging must meet all of the standards defined by local laws and regulations.

 　

◆In addition to compliance with laws and regulations, establish self-imposed or voluntary standards in consideration of market conditions and competing companies.

 　

◆Matter leaching from packaging into product contents must comply with local laws and regulations. (Example: migration test)

 　
b.　Compatibility of primary packaging with contents
Defect mode Organoleptic defect
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
Packaging must not cause properties of product contents to deteriorate.
What/how to achieve

◆Use a primary packaging that matches the contents of the product.

 　
c.　Sealing performance and opening performance
Defect mode Sealing defect / Difficult to open / Microbe contamination
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
Sealing integrity must be ensured for all products in order to protect product contents within the best-before period.
Also consumers can open the product easily.
What/how to achieve

◆There must be no leakage or suction that carries a risk of microbial spoilage.

 　

◆Any other leakage from the body part of the packaging must also be avoided. (Example: pin-holes caused by PET bottle blowing defects, can bodies crazing caused by contact with sodium.)

 　

◆Each company must define standards for opening properties according to what is acceptable for common products in the market where the product is sold, and manage the opening properties to meet those standards.

 　

◆Each company must define standards for tamper-evident properties according to what is acceptable for common products in the market in countries where the product is sold, and manage temper-evident properties to meet those standards.

 　
d.　Safety
Defect mode Scratched packaging which may cause injury
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
Safety of packaging to the customer and consumer must be ensured in any and all cases.
What/how to achieve

◆Do not ship defective products that could injure the consumers. (Example: Defects that cause customer injury, such as broken glass or sharp edged support rings, are avoided.)

 　
e.　Strength
Defect mode Packaging appearance defect / Others
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
All packaging should have sufficient strength.
What/how to achieve

◆Primary and secondary packaging must be designed to be tough enough to withstand stacking on pallets.
