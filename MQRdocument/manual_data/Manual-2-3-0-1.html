1.4.1.2　Design of inspection items before shipment
a.　Design of product inspection items (physical/chemical test, sensory test, microbe test, sealing performance test, filling volume test, visual inspection)
Defect mode 　
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To satisfy the quality standards (document No. Q3WW001) issued by SBF and the specifications in the product specification issued by Region/BU for all finished products to be shipped
What/how to achieve

◆Specify necessary inspection items before decision for product shipping. (At least the physical/chemical test, microbe test, sensory test, visual inspection, and sealing performance test must be performed.)

◆Clearly define the inspection frequency and volume. See the following to determine the frequency and volume.

 　
　

・Physical/chemical and sensory tests: Check for each prepared batch to find any mistake in mixing process and check immediately after the start and at the end to detect any residual water or pipe water contamination.

 　
　

・Microbe test: Check immediately after the start, every few to eight hours, and at the end to detect microbes that increase over time. Run the test multiple times because there are variations in numbers.

 　
　

・Sealing performance test: Check immediately after the start, every few to six hours, and at the end to detect changes over time. Run the test as many times as the number of heads. (All sealing heads: all capping heads, all capping heads, all crowning heads and etc.)

 　
　

・Sealing test: For canned products or retort products containing milk, perform the test before shipment as necessary in consideration of slow leak risks (Example: Case upside down leak test, offline contact pressure/can hammer test).
