2.　Glossary
Defect mode list Gloss
Label error Defects related with labels such as no label, break of label, and doubled labels.
Different packaging 　
Low or excessive filling 　
Lack of products in secondary packaging 　
Usage of restricted ingredients/packaging 　
Others (violation of law) 　
Microbe contamination Defects caused by contamination of microbes
Foreign material contamination Defect products mixed foreign matter such as metal, glass, plastic, insect, gasket, etc.
Mixing different liquid Defect products mixed foreign liquid such as CIP chemical solution, grease, oil, excessive water, other product contents.
Allergen contamination 　
Organoleptic defect Defect of color, taste, flavor, etc.
Sealing defect 　
BBD coding defect Violation of the laws and regulations related with best before date coding
Scratched packaging which may cause injury 　
Packaging appearance defect Packaging appearance defect such as adhesion of foreign matter or dents
Difficult to open 　
Shipping non-conforming products or test sample 　
Others 　
Abbreviations 　
MQR Manufacturing Quality Requirement (This document)
SBF Suntory Beverage & Food Limited
BBD Best Before Date
CIP Cleaning in Place
COP Cleaning out of Place (means that manual cleaning with disassembling the parts and automated / manual cleaning of outer surface of equipment)
OPL One Point Lesson
RGB Returnable Glass Bottle
SIP Sterilization in place (means that sterilizing the equipment for preparation of starting production in the aseptic line)
SOP Standard Operating Procedure
SMW SBF Monozukuri Way
Definitions 　
Contents Product contents such as beverage or syrup
Contents water Water that is used for producing contents
Process water Water that may enter products such as CIP final rinse water, neck rinse water, primary packaging rinse water, etc.
Primary packaging Packaging which directly contact with contents(foods)
Secondary packaging Packaging needed to sell the products excluding primary packaging. For example, labels, carton boxes, shrink wraps, or plastic crate
Hard to clean area(s) Areas or parts which is not able to be completely cleaned by CIP.
Outflow prevention Prevent non-conforming products from being shipped to the market
Target microbe Microbes which can grow under normal storage and distribution conditions and can spoil the contents
Tilting conveyor sterilization Sterilize the inner surface of the primary packaging by tilting it on a conveyor after filling the contents at high temperature in the Hot filling line.
Changeover Change of production condition to produce different SKU
Flavor contamination The odor other than the original odor (e.g., odor of other contents and raw materials, odor of chemicals, odor of mold, etc.) is attached to the content liquid.
Rework Work to reprocess defective products into conforming products
