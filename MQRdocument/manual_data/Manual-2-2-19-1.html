1.3.20.2　Inspection/Rework
a.　Inspection/Rework
Defect mode Packaging appearance defect / Organoleptic defect / Others / Shipping non-conforming products or test sample / Scratched packaging which may cause injury
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To appropriately inspect products offline and perform rework Also, do not cause any secondary damage due to offline product inspection or rework.
What/how to achieve

◆Set the work rules for offline product inspection and rework while considering the following:

 　
　

・Develop an appropriate work environment (lighting, stands, tools, space, human resources and breaks to avoid heavy workload, etc.) and perform the operation in a sanitary environment equivalent to or higher than the product warehouse.

 　
　

・Record 1. The number of products before work, 2. The number of conforming products after work, and 3. The number of non-conforming products after work. Check each against the number of corresponding actual products, and finish the operation after confirming that all match.

 　
　

・Clearly separate storage areas for the above 1 to 3 so that they are not mixed. (See the figure.)

 　
　

・Assign a supervisor to ensure that the worker knows the operation procedure before work (how to tell non-conforming products from conforming products, rework procedure, movement line during work, designating the above 1 to 3, etc.).

 　
　

・Dispose of non-conforming products immediately after work and record the number of them.

 　
　

・Contents must not be reused, or products with sealing defects must not be reworked.

 　
　

・Reworked products must have the same appropriate BBD coding as normal products so that the production status can be traced.

 　
　

・Do not basically use tools that may damage products. (See the figure.) If you have no choice but to use them, clarify the operation procedure to ensure no damage to products before use.

 　
　

・List work risks in advance to prevent quality defects due to offline inspection or rework. (Example: Wearing designated clothes and gloves to prevent hair and nail contamination. Soft handling to prevent damage to products. Clarifying the work to prevent glue/adhesive tape from attaching to products when resealing a carton. Prohibiting the use of staplers to prevent foreign material contamination and pinholes on products)

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Make sure that the number of products to be worked matches the total number of conforming and non-conforming products after work. 　
Frequency or timing After work 　
Operating limit or standard condition The numbers must add up perfectly. The record number must match the actual number.
