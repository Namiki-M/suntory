1.3.1.2　Employee sanitation/Brought-in item management
a.　Employee sanitation management
Defect mode Foreign material contamination / Microbe contamination / Allergen contamination
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To ensure that employees are always sanitized and wear clean cloths appropriately
What/how to achieve

◆Specify a work uniform (cap, clothes, shoes, etc.) to maintain a sanitary environment and to prevent foreign material contamination. (See the figure.)

 　
　

・Use a work uniform that prevents hair and beard contamination and does not cause buttons to fall off.

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Replace the uniform with a clean one specified locally if it is stained during operation. 　
Frequency or timing When the uniform is stained during operation 　
Operating limit or standard condition No stains 　
Figure
b.　Brought-in item management
Defect mode Foreign material contamination
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
To prevent people from bringing in items that may be mixed in products
What/how to achieve

◆Specify items that are prohibited to be brought into locations where risk of foreign material contamination in products exists and post the rules. (See the figure.)

 　
How to maintain/control
Task 1
Procedure Explain the rules about the prohibited items to people who enter the plant. 　
Frequency or timing Before entering locations with restrictions on brought-in items 　
Operating limit or standard condition The predetermined items must not be brought in. 　
Figure
