1.2.2.1　Evaluation procedures for approval
a.　General packaging evaluation procedures
Defect mode Others
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
Reduce the risk of incident caused by packaging by proper evaluation and approval.
What/how to achieve

◆The evaluation items and specifications must be agreed to with the suppliers. Also the specifications defined in section 1.2.1 must be guaranteed.

 　

◆Based on the evaluation results, the responsible division must approve whether the packaging is usable or not. (According to brand classification, the responsible division for giving approval have been defined. See attached table.)

 　

◆Regarding typical changing points and evaluation items, see attached matrix as a reference. Also see the following sections for further details about in each steps.

 　
　

・Second and third steps may be skipped or combined after discussing the impact of the change with division responsible for packaging approval, if there has been no obvious significant change. In that case, the reason why skipped or combined needs to be recorded.

 　
　

・Combinations of packaging and changes not included in the matrix need to be planned for according to the circumstances.

Defect mode Others
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
Conduct offline evaluation for the packaging approval judgement.
What/how to achieve

◆Each company should evaluate the suppliers of packaging, individual items, and combination parameters accordingly. See following tables as a recommendation.

c.　2nd step: Evaluation of small-scale online products
Defect mode Others
Subjected packaging All packaging
Subjected line All lines
Purpose
Make samples on the actual line and evaluate them for the packaging approval judgement.
What/how to achieve

◆Each company should evaluate the combinations of packaging created in a process to check the suitability of the combinations of packaging with the process. See following tables as a recommendation.

 　

◆At the time of evaluation, process conditions must be same as actual production.

 　

◆It is recommended to confirm the suitability of packaging for handling when taking samples by checking for jams, dents, etc.

 　

◆Discuss the number of evaluation samples with division responsible for packaging approval. (Example of scale: approx. 100 units)
